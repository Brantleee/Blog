Leading up to Blog Post One I had no experience with HTML, and now thanks to CS347 I have begun to experiemnt and understand it! During our first few weeks of class we learned a plethora of things, focusing primarily on lists, different types of headers, how to display code, how to link to a website, and how to link to an anchor! The most interesting of these was how to link to an anchor on the current page, as this has so many applications. At first understanding HTML page layouts was confusing, however once I realized the stucture, the difference between a header and head, and why having a seperate body is useful, I quickly advanced my knowledge surrounding HTML. In addition to the material provided in class, I found that it was easy to understand a function of HTML by looking at MDN Web Docs. This, combined with in class activities has kickstarted my HTML heart.
